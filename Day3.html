MUST TRY FILIPINO FOODS

Traveling can be so tiring, and we know that good foods can be our relaxing buddy throughout our journey in exploring the wonders of this Earth. So here are some of the foods that you must try as you visit Philippines. And below this will be my personal thoughts and rating on every food that I will show here so you can have ideas about these foods.

Disclaimer: Some food recommendations bellow contains pork and other haram foods, also beef and other food that might be prohibited to you.

Appetizers

A. Lumpia B. Atsara C. Sisig D. Calamares

Name of Dish Description Taste Nyrle's Rating
A. Lumpia Lumpia has many variant, it depends on its filling. The filling was wrapped using a lumpia wrapper and then it will be deep-fried. Fresh Lumpia is the only lumpia variant that is not fried, it also has a different wrapper and it is just a vegetable with some peanut sauce. Pork, Fish, Chicken, Vegetable, Cheese, and Dynamite (long green chili) are some of its known variant. Crunchy and flavorful depending on the variant 10 if it is freshly cooked
B. Atsara Atsara is made of slightly ripe papaya fruit with some carrots. It has a cooked sauce made of vinegar, sugar, and spices like pepper, salt, onions, bell pepper, and ginger. Some also added other ingredients lite raisin. Perfectly balanced sweet and sour taste 10 if it made by my mother 😅
C. Sisig Sisig has many variant, it depends on the meat that is used. Common variant of sisig is pork, Bangus(Milkfish), chicken, and tofu. It has also two kinds, one with mayonnaise and one has no mayo which is what was claimed to be the original Sisig. The meat was grilled or others fry it to make it crispy. Meats were sliced in a smaller pieces and then put in a pan with other ingredients to cook it together, and then put it in a sizzling plate. Crunchy, flavorful and slightly spicy 10 if it well cooked and prepared. But Tofu Sisig is not for me.
D. Calamares Calamares is a squid coated in a flour batter and a breadcrumbs and then it is fried. It is best when freshly cooked and comes with a good dipping sauce like a perfectly made mayo dip or a spiced vinegar Crunchy and flavorful depending on the variant 10 if it is freshly cooked and comes with good dipping sauce and if I have no allergy with it
